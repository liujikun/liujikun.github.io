Hello I'm

Wei Liu

I am currently a research scientist at Seed Vision team, ByteDance Inc. I received my bachelor and Ph.D. from Harbin Institute of Technology, Harbin, China in 2016 and 2020, respectively. From 2017 to 2019, I was a visiting student at the Ohio State University, Columbus, USA. My main research interests include: Image/Video Generation, Agent and world model.
